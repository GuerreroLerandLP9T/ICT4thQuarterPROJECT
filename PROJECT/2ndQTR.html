TOURIST SPOTS IN THE PHILIPPINES

Beautiful and elegant places! one even recognized as the 7 wonders of the world, Truly philippines has alot to offer and these are some of them!

Tubbataha Reef, Palawan

UNESCO has recognized the Tubbataha Reef in the Philippines as a unique underwater wonder, highlighting the impact of natural wonders.

Mayon Volcano, Bicol

Albay Bicol, province offers a breathtaking view of the Mayon Volcano, the Philippines' most active and perfect volcanic cone.

Fort Santiago

Manila's famous 64-hectare Spanish fortress, built by Miguel Lopez de Legazpi, is a popular Instagrammable location. Despite being destroyed during WWII, its ruins remain, home to numerous institutions, universities, historical sites, and churches.

Kawasan Falls, Cebu

Kawasan Falls in Cebu, known for its turquoise waters, is a popular destination for swimming and hydrotherapy, with the 40-meter-tall first cascade being the most popular spot.
